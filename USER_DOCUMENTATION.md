# National Health Facility Registry (MFL) - User Manual

**National Department of Health - Papua New Guinea**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** December 2025

---

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the **National Health Facility Registry (MFL)**. This platform is the single authoritative source for health facility data in PNG. It allows Ministry staff, partners, and the public to visualize, manage, and verify the status of health services across the country.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Accessing the System
*   **Website**: Navigate to [https://mfl.lamtoninvestments.com](https://mfl.lamtoninvestments.com)
*   **Requirements**: A modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).

### 2.2 Logging In
1.  Enter your **Username/Email** (e.g., `officer@health.gov.pg`).
2.  Enter your **Password**.
3.  Click **Sign In**.
    *   *Note: If you are already logged in, you will be taken directly to the Dashboard.*

### 2.3 The Dashboard Interface
Once logged in, you will see two main areas:
*   **Sidebar (Left)**: Your main navigation menu. It collapses to save space on smaller screens.
*   **Main Workspace (Right)**: Displays the map, forms, or data tables depending on your selection.

---

## 3. Using the Interactive Map

The **Registry Map** is the default landing page.

*   **Navigation**: Click and drag to move the map. Use the `+` and `-` buttons (top-left) or your mouse wheel to zoom in/out.
*   **Filtering**: Use the floating panel on the top-right to filter facilities by:
    *   **Sector** (Government, Church, Private)
    *   **Facility Type** (Hospital, Health Centre, Aid Post, etc.)
    *   **Operational Status** (Functional, Closed, etc.)
*   **Searching**: Click the **Search Icon** (top-right side) to type a facility name. The map will fly to the location selected.
*   **Viewing Details**: Click any facility marker (dot) on the map to open the **Facility Detail Panel**.

---

## 4. Managing Facilities

### 4.1 Viewing Facility Details
From the **Facility Detail Panel** (popup), you can see:
*   **Overview**: Basic details (LLG, Ward, Code).
*   **Contact**: Phone, officer in charge.
*   **Services**: List of medical services provided (e.g., HIV/AIDS, Immunization).
*   **Infrastructure**: Power sources, water supply, and beds.

### 4.2 Editing a Facility
1.  Open a Facility Detail Panel.
2.  Click the **Edit** button (Pencil Icon) in the top-right of the panel.
3.  You will be taken to the **Facility Editor Form**.
4.  Update the fields as necessary (Name, Type, Location, Status).
5.  Click **Submit for Approval**.
    *   *Important:* Changes do NOT go live immediately. They are sent to the **Workflow Console** for review.

### 4.3 Digital Verification & Licenses
*   **QR Code**: In the "Overview" tab, scrolling down reveals a unique **QR Code**. This can be scanned by any smartphone to verify the facility's official status on the public registry.
*   **Print Certificate**: In the Detail Panel header, click the **Certificate/License Icon**. This generates an official A4-size PDF certificate for the facility to print and display.

---

## 5. Workflow & Approvals

For **Approvers** and **Administrators**:

1.  Navigate to **Workflow Console** in the sidebar.
2.  You will see a list of **Pending Submissions**.
3.  Click on a submission to view the **Current Data** vs **Proposed Changes**.
4.  **Action**:
    *   **Approve**: The changes are applied to the live map immediately.
    *   **Reject**: The changes are discarded, and the facility officer is notified.

---

## 6. Analytics Dashboard

Navigate to **Analytics Dashboard** to view high-level summaries:
*   **Facility Coverage**: Charts showing facilities per province/region.
*   **Functional Status**: Percentage of facilities currently operational vs closed.
*   **Infrastructure Gaps**: Analysis of facilities without power or water.
